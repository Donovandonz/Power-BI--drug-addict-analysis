## 📋 Project Overview

A comprehensive data analytics dashboard visualizing drug addiction demographics across **Malaysia** (2019-2023). This project analyzes addiction patterns by age group, education level, occupation, and geographic distribution to provide actionable insights for public health initiatives and policy-making.

## 🔍 Key Age Group Insights

### Critical Findings by Age Group

#### 🚨 Peak Addiction Ages: 25-39 vs 40+
| Age Band | Total Addicts | % of Total | Key Insight |
|----------|--------------|------------|-------------|
| **40+** | 144,642 | 13.46% | Highest single group - long-term addiction burden |
| **25-39** | 398,645 | 37.10% | 37% of all addicts in prime working-age |
| **25-39 + 40+** | **543,287** | **50.56%** | Over half of all addicts are 25+ years old |

#### ⚠️ Youth At Risk: 16-24
| Age Band | Total Addicts | % of Total | Key Insight |
|----------|--------------|------------|-------------|
| **16-24** | 244,929 | 22.80% | Nearly 1 in 4 addicts are youth/young adults |
| 20-24 | 129,111 | 12.02% | Post-secondary/early career |
| 16-19 | 115,818 | 10.78% | School-going age |

#### 🛡️ Children & Teens: Under 16
| Age Band | Total Addicts | % of Total | Key Insight |
